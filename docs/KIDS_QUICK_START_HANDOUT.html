Operator Rules

Every good radio operator misses signals sometimes. The trick is to slow down, try again, and keep your rhythm steady.

Do not rush. Clean signals beat fast guesses.
Use Clear when you want a fresh try.
Daily complete? Try Words or Signal Sprint for extra practice.
Practice time matters. Your brain gets stronger with repeats.
Your mission: five good minutes today. Small practice, repeated often, turns dots and dashes into a language you can hear.
